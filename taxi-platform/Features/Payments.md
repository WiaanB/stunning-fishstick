# Payments

## TLDR
Wallet top-up payment rail for the platform, using bank-to-bank transfer rather than cards.

## Capabilities
- **PayShap** selected as primary payment rail for wallet top-ups: bank-to-bank, no card required, broad SA bank support
- Airtime-as-payment considered and ruled out due to carrier billing restrictions

## Status
